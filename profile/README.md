## ğŸ¥ About Anamnetic

**Anamnetic** is a digital health assistant designed to bridge the gap between patients and their healthcare journeys. In a world where health data is fragmented across clinics, devices, and paper records, Anamnetic provides a centralized, secure, and intelligent platform for health management.

The solution acts as a personal health hub, tracking:
* **Medical Consultations:** History, notes, and professional advice.
* **Exams & Diagnostics:** Centralized storage for lab results and imaging.
* **Personal Metrics:** Real-time data integration from smartwatches (heart rate, sleep, steps) and digital scales (body composition).
* **Treatments:** Monitoring medication adherence and long-term health plans.
